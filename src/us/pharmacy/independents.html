Frequently asked

Common questions from
independent owners.

Do I need to switch from PioneerRx?

No. MedMe runs alongside your existing pharmacy management system. Your dispensing workflow stays exactly the same. MedMe handles the clinical encounter and medical billing side only.

Can I add billing later if I start with the Clinical Starter tier?

Yes. Adding Medical Billing later is a single-toggle change. No data migration, no platform switch, no new contract. Your historical clinical data carries forward.

How much does it cost per store?

Flat monthly per location, billed quarterly or annually. The full breakdown is on our pricing page. Most independents pay back the platform fee on their first month's CCM billing alone.

What if I have one tech who handles all my back-office work?

That's most of our independent customers. MedMe is designed so a single non-clinical staff member can manage the queue, monitor claims, and handle denials. We train them directly.

Are there long-term contracts?

12-month minimum term, renewing month-to-month afterward. We're confident enough in the platform that if you cancel in the first 90 days, we refund the implementation fee.
