Three commercial models

Structure the economics
your way.

We've worked with networks of every shape. Pick the model that gets your members to yes.

Model 1

Member discount

The network negotiates a preferred rate; members subscribe directly at the discounted price. Lowest lift for the network office, fastest to stand up.

Model 2

Volume rebate

Members subscribe at standard rates; the network earns a rebate scaled to total adoption. Aligns the network's incentive with member uptake.

Model 3

Network-paid subscription

The network funds the platform centrally as a member benefit. Maximum adoption, fully standardized, and the strongest data position network-wide.
