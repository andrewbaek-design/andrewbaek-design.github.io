Why MedMe

Pharmacy tech ends where
clinical work begins.

Your dispensing stack is solid. The clinical side is where everything falls apart, and that's exactly where the growth is.

Clinical services live outside your tech stack

Vaccines, MTM, point-of-care testing, your team is already delivering the work. But the documentation, scheduling, and follow-up live in spreadsheets, paper, and disconnected portals.

Your PMS dispenses; nothing else fits

Your pharmacy management system runs the fill. It was never built for booking visits, drafting SOAP notes, tracking encounters, or submitting medical claims.

AI is everywhere, integrated nowhere

Every vendor demos an AI tool. Most can't see your patients, your dispensing history, or your scheduled visits, so the agents can't act on what's actually happening in your pharmacy.

Scaling clinical services means restarting

Every new store, every new program, every new payer launches like it's the first time. No standardized playbook. No infrastructure that travels with you.
