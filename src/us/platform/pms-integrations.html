Our integration approach

Why none of this requires
a six-month IT project.

Pre-built, not bespoke

Every integration listed is shipped as a managed connector. No custom engineering, no third-party integration tax, no project plan that lives in someone's email.

Bi-directional sync

Patient records flow from your PMS into MedMe. Encounter outcomes and codes flow back. Nothing duplicated, nothing manually re-keyed.

HL7 + FHIR + custom

HL7v2 for legacy. FHIR for modern. SFTP for batch. REST API for everything else. If your stack speaks data, we connect to it.
