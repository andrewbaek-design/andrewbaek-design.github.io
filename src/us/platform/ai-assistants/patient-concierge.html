What It Does

Three jobs your team
will never call back about.

Patient Concierge takes the routine touchpoints off your team's plate so they can focus on dispensing and clinical care.

  • Answers inbound phone calls and handles inquiries: refills, store hours, appointment booking and more.
  • Automates outbound phone calls to coordinate medication delivery, scale treatment adherence checks, post-consultation follow ups and more.
  • Deploys digital chat agents for 24/7 website support, instantly answering patient questions and routing complex inquiries to your team.
Three Channels, One Coordinator

One agent across every channel
