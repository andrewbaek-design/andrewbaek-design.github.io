What Patient Concierge Does

Inbound, outbound,
and the coordination work in between.

Most of pharmacy's operational time goes to high-volume patient touchpoints that do not require clinical judgment. Patient Concierge handles those touchpoints by voice or chat, hands the exceptions to a human, and routes every conversation back into MedMe so the chart stays current.

Inbound

Patient and provider inquiries

Voice and chat handling for the everyday operational calls. Status checks, navigation, FAQs, with multi-language support across 30+ languages. Escalates to a human the moment clinical judgment is required.

Outbound

Refill reminders & adherence

Proactive patient outreach at the right cadence. Refill check-ins, treatment adherence nudges, delivery coordination, and follow-up. Replies route back into the patient's chart, not into a separate SMS tool.

Coordination

Payer & PA workflows

Status follow-ups on prior authorizations, documentation requests, and payer status checks. Automated where they can be, structured where they can't.
