How It Works

Automated data entry
that works like a real clerk.

Pharmacy back-office runs on systems built before the integration era. Admin Clerk operates against those systems the way a careful clerk would, with screens, rules, and a paper trail.

  • Reads your screen using OCR to extract data from faxes, portals, and any interface a clerk would read.
  • Applies pharmacy-specific business rules: brand vs generic, on-hand inventory, fill timing, controlled-substance handling.
  • Automates when confident and routes exceptions to a pharmacist for review, with a complete audit trail of every action taken.
  • Runs continuously in the background or overnight, processing the queue while your team is closed.
Sample Workflow

Fax-to-Rx entry,
end to end.

Six steps. No clicks from your team. Admin Clerk monitors the inbox, classifies docs, matches patient and provider, picks the right drug, applies your business logic, and fills in the Rx details, then routes anything ambiguous to a pharmacist for review.

01

Monitor PMS fax inbox for new docs

Polls the fax inbox on a default 3-minute cadence; tunable to your traffic profile.

02

Classify Document

Only processes designated document classes such as Rxs and transfers; everything else is set aside.

03

Patient & Provider Match

Looks up the patient and prescriber in your PMS, or creates a new record if no match is found.

04

Drug Selection

Picks the right NDC based on your business rules: brand vs generic preferences, on-hand inventory, formulary.

05

Additional Business Logic

Applies fill-timing rules, controlled-substance handling, prior-auth flags, and any other workflow logic your pharmacy enforces.

06

Fill Rx Details

SIG, dispensed quantity, refills, day supply, and any remaining fields, ready for pharmacist verification.
