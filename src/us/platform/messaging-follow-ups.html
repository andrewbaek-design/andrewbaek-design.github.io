Patient Conversation, Without the Phone Calls

Reminders, recall, follow-up.
Three jobs, one place.

Patients prefer text over phone calls. Pharmacy teams are the most-called part of the system. Messaging closes that gap: every routine touchpoint runs by SMS or email, with two-way reply flowing back into the patient's chart and the pharmacist's queue.

Reminders

Appointment + refill reminders

24-hour and 1-hour appointment reminders. Refill check-ins 7 days and 2 days before each fill. Adherence nudges scheduled by therapy class. Two-way reschedule routes the rebook back into the scheduler.

Recall

Bring-back campaigns

Identify patients due for CCM, MTM, A1C, BP check-ins, or seasonal vaccines. Send a campaign to the cohort, accept replies into the scheduler, and watch encounters book without a phone call. 240 booked follow-ups in month one is typical.

Follow-Ups

Post-encounter follow-up

Care plan check-ins, adherence prompts, and education content delivered on the cadence each protocol requires. Replies route into the patient's chart for the pharmacist to review, not into a separate SMS inbox.
