What It Does

Transcribes consultations.
Structures data. Generates billable docs.

Transcribes consultations, structures data in real time, and generates compliant, billable documentation instantly, turning what once took minutes into seconds. The pharmacist retains full control to review, edit, and approve every record, maintaining clinical integrity while eliminating repetitive manual entry.

Listens

Captures the consultation in real time with on-device speech recognition tuned to pharmacy vocabulary, drugs, doses, and clinical entities.

Transcribes

Structures the conversation into a clinical record as it happens. Drugs, doses, adherence concepts, and care-plan elements tagged automatically.

Auto-fills

Populates the documentation form, compliant, billable, and ready for pharmacist review and sign-off in seconds, not after-hours.

Clinical Assistant Use Cases

Five places it lifts the load,
so your pharmacist can keep moving.

From routine documentation to high-stakes communication summaries, Clinical Assistant slots into the pharmacy workflow wherever a pharmacist would otherwise be re-typing what they just said out loud.

Medication-error or incident reporting

Capture the incident as it's discussed, structure it for the reporting form, ready for pharmacist review.

Clinical assessment documentation

SOAP-style notes auto-populated from the consultation. Pharmacist edits the draft, not the blank page.

Prescriber communication summaries

Therapy updates, side-effect escalations, and other prescriber-facing summaries drafted from the visit transcript.

Drafting follow-up action plans

Next-step plans and patient-facing action items written up automatically, ready for review and send.

Care-gap closure tasks

Drafting care-gap closure tasks based on patient responses surfaced during the consultation, structured for follow-up.
