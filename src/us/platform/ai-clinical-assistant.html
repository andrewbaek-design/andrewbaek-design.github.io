How it works

Four steps from waveform
to signed encounter.

1

Listen

Tap "Start Scribe" on your device. AI Scribe listens to the encounter, in-room or telephone, in HIPAA-compliant ambient mode.

2

Draft

A pharmacy-tuned LLM drafts the SOAP note in real time. Templates auto-select based on the visit type and patient history.

3

Suggest

CPT/HCPCS codes, modifiers, and diagnosis pointers are suggested based on what was said. Time tracking auto-stamps.

4

Sign

You review, edit if needed, and sign. The encounter is filed, the code is set, and the billing chain takes over.
