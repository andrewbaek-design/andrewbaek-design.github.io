The Five-Step Billing Chain

From appointment scheduled
to claim paid, automated end to end.

No spreadsheets. No re-keying. No outsourced biller you have never met. Every step is captured in MedMe and surfaced in one billing console.

01

Eligibility check

Real-time Medicare Part B and commercial-payer eligibility runs the moment a patient books. Pharmacists never deliver a service the payer will not cover.

02

Smart coding

As the SOAP note is written, MedMe suggests the correct CPT/HCPCS code, the right modifier, and the diagnosis pointer.

03

Time tracking

Time-based services like 99490 and 99607 are tracked automatically in the background, producing a defensible audit trail.

04

Claim submission

Clean 837P claims pushed to Change Healthcare, Availity, or Office Ally, pre-validated against payer-specific rules.

05

Denial management

Denials are routed by reason code. MedMe pre-fills the corrected claim, drafts the appeal, and tracks the resubmission until the dollar lands.

Where MedMe Wins

Three places generic
billing tools fall apart.

Eligibility

Live 270/271 verification

Patient eligibility, copay, deductible-met, plan type, and CCM-already-billed-this-month, all surfaced before the encounter starts. No more delivering a service to a patient who already had it billed elsewhere.

Smart Coding

Pharmacy-tuned code engine

Encounter type plus time plus diagnosis plus pharmacist credential gives MedMe enough signal to suggest the correct primary code, add-on codes, and modifiers, and to flag when an encounter should be coded as 99491 instead of 99490.
