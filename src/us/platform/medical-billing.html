Frequently asked

Questions we hear
before signing.

Do we need to switch clearinghouses?

No. MedMe pushes clean 837P claims to whichever clearinghouse you already use — Change Healthcare, Availity, Office Ally, Waystar are pre-integrated. If you have a private biller, we push via API or SFTP.

What payers are supported beyond Medicare?

Every commercial payer with an eligibility endpoint. Custom payer rules — place-of-service, modifier behavior, prior-auth requirements — are configurable per store. We also support Medicaid managed-care and dual-eligible billing flows.

Does MedMe replace our existing pharmacy management system?

No. MedMe runs alongside PioneerRx, BestRx, McKesson, and other pharmacy management systems via our integration layer. Your dispensing workflow is untouched. MedMe handles only the clinical encounter + medical billing side.

How long until first paid claim?

Median 30 days from contract signed to first billable encounter. The fastest customers were live in 11 days. Your timeline depends on Medicare enrollment status, state scope-of-practice setup, and clearinghouse credentialing — all of which we walk you through.

Can I see what we'd unlock before signing?

Yes. Our reimbursement calculator estimates your annual Part B revenue based on your encounter volume and service mix. A 15-minute conversation gives you a written estimate with confidence ranges.

What about HIPAA and SOC 2?

HIPAA-compliant from day one. SOC 2 Type II audited annually. BAA signed at contract. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We pass enterprise health-system security reviews regularly.
