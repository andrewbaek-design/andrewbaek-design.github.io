What Intake Captures

Built for the encounter,
not for a generic form library.

Every intake form is service-specific and matches the documentation the encounter actually requires. CCM consent on a CCM intake. VIS attestation on a vaccine intake. Medication lists on an MTM intake. The pharmacist walks into the visit ready to deliver care, not gather paperwork.

Service-Specific

Templates that match the visit

CCM consent forms, MTM medication reconciliation lists, VIS attestation for vaccines, PrEP risk screening, contraception eligibility. Every service runs its own intake template.

Mobile-First

60 seconds on the patient's phone

SMS-delivered link opens on any phone, no app install. Mobile-first design with 12-language support. 68% of patients complete intake in under 90 seconds before they arrive.

Eligibility-Aware

Verified before the visit

Eligibility data captured during booking flows into the intake form alongside the patient's responses. Payer, copay, plan type, and CCM-this-month status surface on the chart at start-of-visit.
